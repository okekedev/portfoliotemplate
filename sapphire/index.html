What is Sapphires?

Sapphires is a Business Operating System (BOS) — a single platform that runs the day-to-day across every department of your company. Marketing, sales, operations, administration, billing, and reception each get their own AI head with its own tools, context, and accountability.

Think of it as the operating system your business has been missing: one place where workflows, integrations, voice, email, and data all live, instead of stitched together across a dozen SaaS tabs.

Built for businesses ready to scale without doubling headcount.

🏛️

One AI Per Department

Each department head is self-sufficient with its own toolset. No central bottleneck, no cross-tab handoffs.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Granular permissions per member, per department. System and custom roles, scoped to each business.

📞

Voice & Email Built In

Inbound call screening, outbound calling, scheduled email — all native to the platform.

🔌

Bring Your Own Accounts

Connect Stripe, Twilio, and ad platforms with your own credentials. We don't take custody of your data.
