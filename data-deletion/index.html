Data Deletion

How to request deletion of your data from Okeke LLC websites and applications.

Overview

If you have used one of our applications and would like to request deletion of your personal data, you can do so by contacting us. We are committed to respecting your privacy and process deletion requests promptly.

What Gets Deleted

When you request deletion, we remove:

  • Your account information (name, email, business details)
  • All OAuth tokens and credentials for any third-party platforms you connected (Meta, LinkedIn, Google, Stripe, etc.) — tokens are revoked and the encrypted records are purged from our systems
  • Business and prospect data you entered into the platform
  • AI-generated reports, analyses, and recommendations
  • Email and post-publishing history associated with your account
  • Usage logs and analytics tied to your account

If you have only used the website (and have not signed in to any of our apps), there is no account data to delete. Anonymous website analytics are not linked to identifiable individuals.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to contact@okeke.us with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will confirm receipt and complete the deletion within 30 days.

Revoking Platform Access on Your Own

You can also revoke our apps' access to your connected accounts directly with each platform at any time:

  • Facebook / Instagram: Settings & Privacy → Settings → Apps and Websites → remove the Okeke LLC app
  • LinkedIn: Me → Settings & Privacy → Data Privacy → Permitted Services → remove the Okeke LLC app
  • Google: myaccount.google.com → Security → Third-party apps with account access
  • Stripe: Stripe Dashboard → Settings → Connect → remove the connected app

Revoking on the platform invalidates the OAuth token we hold. Sending a deletion request to us additionally removes the associated records from our systems.

Retention After Deletion

After processing your deletion request, we may retain certain data for up to 90 days in encrypted backups before it is permanently purged. We may also retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you.

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion, contact us at contact@okeke.us or review our Privacy Policy.
