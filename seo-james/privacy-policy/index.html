Privacy Policy

SEO James by Okeke LLC — Last updated: February 18, 2026

1. Introduction

SEO James ("the Platform") is an AI-powered marketing intelligence platform operated by Okeke LLC ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and related services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and business details you provide when creating an account.
  • OAuth Data: When you connect third-party accounts (Google Analytics, Search Console, Facebook, Instagram), we receive access tokens and the analytics data you authorize us to view. We do not store your passwords for these services.
  • Business Data: Information about your business, prospects, website content, and marketing campaigns that you input into the platform.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the platform, including features used, pages viewed, and actions taken.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies necessary for platform functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the SEO James platform.
  • Generate AI-powered marketing analysis, recommendations, and reports for your business.
  • Send outreach emails on your behalf when you authorize such actions.
  • Improve and personalize your experience.
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and support.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Third-Party Services

SEO James integrates with third-party services including Google (Analytics, Search Console, Imagen API), Meta (Facebook, Instagram), SendGrid (email delivery), and Applyra (App Store Optimization). Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We only access the data you explicitly authorize through OAuth consent screens or API configuration.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist us in operating the platform (e.g., cloud hosting, email delivery).
  • AI Providers: Anonymized or necessary data sent to AI models (Claude, Gemini) to generate analysis. These providers process data per their own privacy policies and do not retain your business data.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption of sensitive data at rest and in transit, secure API key storage, and access controls. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 9).

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Export your data in a portable format.

9. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at contact@okeke.us or by visiting our Data Deletion page. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

SEO James is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
