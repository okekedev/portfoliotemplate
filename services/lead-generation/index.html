Lead Generation & Prospect Research

AI-powered prospect discovery that finds, qualifies, and scores leads automatically — so you spend time on prospects that actually convert.

How It Works

01

Discover Prospects

Enter a search query like "plumbers in Austin TX" and our AI scrapes Google Maps to find businesses matching your criteria. Get names, addresses, phone numbers, and websites automatically.

02

Analyze Websites

Each prospect's website is automatically analyzed for quality, mobile responsiveness, SEO issues, and improvement opportunities. Businesses with poor websites become hot leads.

03

Score & Prioritize

AI assigns a lead score based on website quality, business type, location, and likelihood to convert. Focus your outreach on the highest-value prospects first.

04

Export & Act

Export qualified leads to your CRM or pass them directly to our outreach automation. Every lead includes contact info, website analysis, and recommended talking points.

Why AI-Powered Lead Gen?

Manual prospecting is slow and inconsistent. You spend hours searching Google Maps, checking websites, and guessing which leads are worth pursuing.

Our platform automates the entire discovery process. In the time it takes to manually find 10 prospects, our AI can find, analyze, and score hundreds — with better accuracy than human review.

  • Find prospects 10x faster than manual search
  • Never miss a lead in your target market
  • Consistent scoring eliminates guesswork
  • Integrates with website generation & outreach
